1. Making a Reservation

ğŸ® Cultural Context

In Japan, you can make hotel reservations by phone, online, or in person. Many hotels have English-speaking staff, especially in major tourist areas. When making a reservation, be prepared to provide your name, dates, number of guests, and any special requests. It's common to pay a deposit or provide credit card information to secure your reservation. Some traditional Japanese inns (ryokan) may have specific rules or customs, so it's good to ask about them in advance.
