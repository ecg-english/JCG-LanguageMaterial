1. Making an Appointment

ğŸ® Cultural Context

In Japan, hair salons and beauty services are very popular and professional. It's common to make appointments in advance, especially for popular salons. Many salons have English-speaking staff, especially in major cities. When making an appointment, you'll need to specify the type of service you want (cut, color, perm, etc.) and your preferred stylist if you have one. Some salons offer online booking systems, while others prefer phone reservations.

Japanese salons are known for their attention to detail and customer service.
